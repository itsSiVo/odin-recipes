Pad Thai

Simple and versatile dish. Can be used with any protein and can also be vegetarian.

Ingredients

Things you will need:

  • 1 package rice noodles
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halved and cubed
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbs white sugar
  • 2 tbs fish sauce
  • 1 tbs white wine vinegar
  • 1/8 tbs crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft, 30 to 50 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat butter in a wok; add chicken and saute until browned. Removed chicken and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in the wok over medium-high heat. Crack eggs into hot oil and cook until firm. Stir in chicken and cook for 5 minutes. Add softened noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper; mix well until noodles are tender. Adjust seasonings to tastes. Stir in bean sprouts and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with green onions, crushed peanuts, and a wedge of lemon.
