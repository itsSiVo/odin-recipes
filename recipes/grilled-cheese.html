Grilled Cheese Toastie

Easy to make. You have no reason not to try.

Ingridients:

Things you'll need:

  • 4 Slices white bread
  • 3 tbs butter
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Instuctions

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of slice beard.
  3. Place bread on skillet.
  4. Add cheese.
  5. Place second slice of bread on top.
  6. Cook until brown, then turn over sandwich. Continue to cook until brown.
  7. Remove and serve.
