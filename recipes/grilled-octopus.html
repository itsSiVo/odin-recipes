Grilled Octopus

Ingredients

Easy grilled octopus recipe.

Great with a glass of wine on a summer evening.

What you'll need:

  • 2 pounds octopus
  • Olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to season

Instructions

  1. Place octopus in a pot and cover with enough water. Bring to a boil. Boil for 40 minutes.
  2. Remove octopus from hot water, rinse and place in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and chopped garlic. Let cool at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Preheat grill to a medium-high heat.
  4. Slice octopus tentacles and grill for 3-4 minutes per side, until charred.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and add lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add oregana and parsley to taste.
